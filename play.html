Field of View

It's 11 p.m. on a Friday night in August. After a long day at work and an enjoyable dinner with friends, you're on your way home. Your favorite scarf is wrapped around your neck, and you're feeling good.

Your heart beats, pulling you home.

You make it home at last and slam the door shut behind you. It's a scary world out there...

Epilogue

You flee in a blind panic, the racing of your heart drowning out all else. Everything goes dark... and you awaken on your doorstep, having made it home somehow.

You look around, but home no longer feels as safe as it used to.
